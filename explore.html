Explore The Sandvika Science Museum

Take a step back in time and become a paleontologist for a day! Our Dinosaur Expedition exhibit features life-sized dinosaur replicas, fossil dig sites, and a chance to uncover the secrets of these magnificent creatures. Kids can even participate in a mock excavation to find their very own dinosaur fossils.
