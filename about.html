about us

We are a community-driven museum that aims to bring science to life through exhibits, events and activities. We believe science should not be confined to the textbook, but brought to live through exhibits. That why w are here to get together to build a communiity museum! As a community-driven museum, we want to work with schools to create places of learning and exploring.
