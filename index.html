Exihibition

Cosmology

May 16 - June 16, 2024

Explore the wonders of our cosmos. Our fantastic exhibition, ‘The Sky Above Us’, explores the night sky and what we can see and know about the universe around us. We’ll locate the various constellations andgalaxies that can be seen and learn a bit about the early navigators who used the stars to travel by.
Read more...

Evolution

May 16 - June 16, 2024

Discover the origins of life on this planet and how the species we know today have evolved our time. You can also take some time to meet a few of the creatures who didn’t survive, including our Woolly Mammoth, the Dodos,and a number of dinosaurs too.

Biology and Medicine

May 16 - June 16, 2024

From micro-organisms to the human body, major breakthroughs in biology are offering us unique insights into the great wonders of the tiny world.For many people their life expectancy is much longer and their quality of life much improved,thanks to the growth of our understanding of medicine.

AI Robotics

May 16 - June 16, 2024

The information revolution is here and robotics and artificial intelligence are the science of the future. From useful home applications of AI to industrial uses of robotics, the future is here. You can even say hello and shake the hand of Rob the Robot.
